PhD student. Working in various digital humanities projects at the Centre for Information Modelling at the University of Graz. I studied history and digital heritage studies (EuroMACHS).
My PhD is about "Information-based resource discovery for historical information. Using the example of historical financial documents". I am very interested in semantic technologies, linked open data and web development in the humanities.
I grew up in a village in Upper Styria in Austria. Together with Christian Steiner I founded a DH/IT company - Digital Humanities Craft - in 2022
